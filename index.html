Frequently Asked Questions

What is a wheel picker?

A wheel picker is an online tool that helps you make random selections or decisions by spinning a virtual wheel. You can add custom options to the wheel, spin it, and let chance decide the outcome.

How do I use the Yes/No wheel?

To use the Yes/No wheel, simply click the 'Yes/No' button at the top of the page, then click the 'SPIN' button in the center of the wheel. The wheel will spin and randomly land on either 'Yes' or 'No' to help you make a decision.

Can I create a custom wheel with my own options?

Yes! You can create a custom wheel by adding your own options in the 'Add Options' section below the wheel. Type your option in the text field and click the '+' button. You can add as many options as you want, and each will be assigned a different color on the wheel.

Is Fortune Twister free to use?

Yes, Fortune Twister is completely free to use with unlimited options and spins. There are no hidden fees or premium features.

Can I use Fortune Twister for classroom activities?

Absolutely! Fortune Twister is perfect for classroom activities like randomly selecting students, assigning tasks, or creating educational games. Teachers love using our random name picker for fair and engaging student selection.

How does the random selection work?

Fortune Twister uses a fair and unbiased random algorithm to determine where the wheel stops. Each option has an equal chance of being selected, ensuring completely random results every time.

Can I save my custom wheel for later use?

Currently, custom wheels are not saved between sessions. However, we're working on adding this feature in a future update to allow users to save and share their custom wheels.

Does Fortune Twister work on mobile devices?

Yes! Fortune Twister is fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Enjoy the same great experience no matter what device you're using.
